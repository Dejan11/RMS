Demo - Mapa rute za kamionski transport tereta | Route map for fraight transport of cargo

Prikaz loše i dobre rute između dve destinacije preko online mape za kamione gde jedino mogu da prevezu robu uzevši u obzir sve moguće zabrane.
Na osnovu precizno planirane rute na našoj aplikaciji dobijamo mogućnost kilometraže uz pomoć koje možemo da formiramo cenovnik za različit tip vozila (solo kamioni, kombi, šleperi,...)

Showing the wrong and legal route between two destinations via an online map for fraight vehicles where they can only transport goods, taking into account all possible prohibitions.
Based on the precisely planned route on our application, we get the option of kilometers with the help of which we can create a price list for different types of vehicles (solo trucks, vans, tow trucks,...)
